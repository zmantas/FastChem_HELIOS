This folder contains calculations for the L5 brown dwarf from Mark Marley's Sonora grid.
There are three different sets of calculations:

without condensation:
chemistry.dat
monitor.dat

equilibrium condensation:
chemistry_cond.dat
condensates.dat
monitor_cond.dat

rainout condensation:
chemistry_rainout.dat
condensates_rainout.dat
monitor_rainout.dat
